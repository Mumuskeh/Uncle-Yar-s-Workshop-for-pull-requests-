-Patches:

Mags Redux - Extra Mags
This will add two magazines: one AUG and one SIG, both chambered for .300. Normal AUG and SG550 mags can be retooled into them.
These may be used by other mods.


There are patches in other modules for new guns, intended to give the ammo some more use. You require the original mods, but please note these files where made over the patches included in this module.

"Hisalute's Weapon Packs - Fixes and Tweaks" already includes a patch for its SCAR-L with a a modified model.
