-Module:
All Ammo Tracers ON [Markuzkiller]

----------------------------------------------------------
-Compatibility:
The original mod isn't needed.

----------------------------------------------------------
-Explanation:

It ensures buckshot don't get tracers as intended by the original mod, and covers a number of ammo mods: Boomsticks and Sharpsticks, Ilrathcxv's 9x21 fmj and hp, Momopate's 20x70 slugs, LEWD's .50 BMG, Nhenhecas's Birdshot, Pillii's 300 Blackout, RonMax's Small Caliber Ammo Framework, and Joseph Porta's Speedloaders and ammo for revolvers.
