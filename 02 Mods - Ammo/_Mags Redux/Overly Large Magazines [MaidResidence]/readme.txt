-Module:
Overly Large Magazines [MaidResidence]

----------------------------------------------------------
-Compatibility:
Original mod not required.

Includes textures, so as to not be dependant from other mods (ie: BaS).

: the mods won't clash, but the drummed Saigas will use the USAS12 drum, not the Saiga drum in this mod.

----------------------------------------------------------
-Explanation:

Not a patch. A collection of large magazines by Maid, that wasn't available in Moddb before, but some mods would use.

Some tweaks and fixes have been made to internal IDs, names, descriptions, and mag qualities. For example, the pistol drums are treated as medium sized mags, not large.

It includes:
- Glock extended magazine, 50 rounds, 9x19, retooled to 9x18.
- Saiga-12 drum magazine, 20-rounds.
- Vepr-12 drum magazine, 20-rounds.
- 7.62x51 drum magazine, 50 rounds, hinge (ie: FAL), retooled to STANAG (ie: SCAR).
- PM drum, 84 rounds, 9x18, retooled to 9x19, craftable*.
- MP5 drum, 50 rounds, 9x19, retooled to 9x18.
- MP7 extended** and short mags, 60 and 20, 9x19, retooled to 9x18.
- STANAG extended casket magazine***, 80 rounds, 5.56x45, retooled to AUG, G36, and SIG.
- AK drum magazine, 75 rounds, 5.45x39, retooled to 7.62x39.
- RPK drum magazine, 95 rounds, 7.62x39, retooled to 5.45x39 and 5.56x45.

*   Compatible with Banjaji's Crafting System Improvements.
**  Originally 30 rounds, but the default MP7 mag loads 40 rounds, so where's the extension there? :_D
*** Originally 100 rounds, but that feels too much for a casket, and the base casket is 60 rounds.

The Saiga and Vepr drums can be retooled between each other. They also were using a extended Saiga mag model. This model will be assigned to the base extended mag (which uses the model for the default mag), and the drums will use the USAS12 drum model.

The PM drum now has a world model taken from Misc. Weapon Pack.

If you use Misc. Weapon Pack, with or witout "Hisalute's Weapon Packs - Fixes and Tweaks", its Vepr drum will be removed in favor of this one.
