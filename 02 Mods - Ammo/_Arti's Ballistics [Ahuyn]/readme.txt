-7.62x39mm Air Burst, from the film Elysium
It (attempts to) recreate the 7.62x39mm Airburst rounds from the film Elysium. These are identical to normal 7.62x39 FMJ rounds, except that in contact they explode with a 1 meter radius.
Initially intended as an addon for JMerc's AK-Elysium Assault Rifle, this module nerfs its stats to that of a normal AK-47, instead of being a one shot kill weapon.
And of course, makes it so it can be used by all vanilla 7.62x3 guns and the few modded ones I've found.

Any 7.62 weapon can load this round, but it degrades your gun hard, only Nimble sells half a mag worth before restock) and crafting them is costly.
It is possible some NPCs have it loaded in their 7.62x39 weapon, little (more like hard and tedious) I can do about it if they have a random ammo load assigned in the loadouts files... which means you may want to worry about how to survive rather than looting it. Be careful!

Do note the rounds in the movie are supposed to explode at about one meter from the target (that's what the computer box attached on top of the AK-47 is supposed to be for)  with a 5~ meter radius.
Ideally that'd be how it works for JMerc's AK-Elysium and other possible high tech 7.62 rifles, but I'm useless with scripting, so at the moment it just explodes as a cluster on contact with a smaller boom.
