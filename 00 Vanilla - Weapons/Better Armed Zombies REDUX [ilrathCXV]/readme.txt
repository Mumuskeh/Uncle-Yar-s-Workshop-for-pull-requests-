-Module:
Better Armed Zombies REDUX [ilrathCXV]

----------------------------------------------------------
-Compatibility:
The original mod isn't needed.

----------------------------------------------------------
-Explanation:

DLTX conversion, with two variants:

Only inheritances: zombies will inherit everything from every other faction and rank. Targeted additions from other mods are kept.

Tabula rasa: only inheritances are in effect. This means additions from mods targeted for zombies are prevented, but ensures a completely equal chance of getting any weapon.

Not many mods bother to give zombies weapons, so I'd just use the second option.
