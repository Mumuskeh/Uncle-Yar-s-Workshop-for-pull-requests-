-Module:
New gas masks [Shiro Usagi]

----------------------------------------------------------
-Compatibility:
The original mod is not needed. The author left open permissions to work with the mod.

----------------------------------------------------------
-Explanation:

IDs have been renamed, for slightly better inventory ordering. This is mostly for the benefit of keeping together some masks like the S10 and MF10 (a variant of the S10), or the Sphere-23M with the vanilla Sphere-08.

All masks now have parts assigned to them. I understand nothing about gas masks, so do tell if something needs changing.

Some text editing for better flow. In particular, the Sangur-V is treated as a fully zonecrafted mask based on the GP-7, but there's an actual MB-T Sangur-V series of Soviet prototypes based on the PMK. So it's now treated as such, to be exact a custom sample.

The Sphere-23M now acts as an inferior Sphere-08, with its ballistic protection instead of that of the ACH-7EX headgear.
