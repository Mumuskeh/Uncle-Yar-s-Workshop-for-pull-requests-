-Explanation:
DLTX conversion, with minor fixes.

I don't carry over the Nosorog/Spartan nerf and Apocalypse buff. While I understand Drunk's rationale, it seems appropiate for Gamma or Separated Helmets games but unneeded in vanilla games, nerfing nosorogs from what advantage they have over normal exos. Of course, that means Gamma and Separated Helmet players may want to opt out of using this.

Reworked text file to not overwrite others.
Reworked icon file for DX8-9 compliance.

-Patches:

CS Helmet
Adds the iconic Clear Sky helmet to the game. Various vanilla Clear Sky NPCs will wear it, and Spore will sell it.
Includes integrated support for Headgear Animations & FDDA Redone 
CSVars Player Outfit Addon includes similar functionality. If you use it, you may skip this patch to avoid redundant items.

CS Helmet - DICK patch
DLTX conversion of the original patch. Ensures the drop of the heavy helmet by vanilla Clear Sky NPCs.
It requires the patch above.

Optimized Vanilla Anomaly Scripts
Merges the changes of both mods.
