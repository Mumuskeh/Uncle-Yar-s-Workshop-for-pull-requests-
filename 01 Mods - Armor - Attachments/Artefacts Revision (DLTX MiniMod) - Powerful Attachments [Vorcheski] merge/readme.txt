-Module:
Artefacts Revision (DLTX MiniMod) [Demonized] - Powerful Attachments [vorcheski] merge

----------------------------------------------------------
-Compatibility:
Original mods not required.

----------------------------------------------------------
-Explanation:
MMR has bigger buffs, but lacks some that PA does. This file merges them so no value is left behind and reduces the number of files to a single one. Might need some further work to standardize values between the two, though.

Also removed artefact bleeding buffs in favor of PA's, retains only the Oasis Heart healing.

You can also choose for an option with DLTX MiniMod's Hardcore Death Lamp.


From the original readmes:

-Artefacts Revision [Demonized]
My little revision of mutant hides and artefacts. Oasis Heart gives about 30-40% more health regeneration, bleeding control artefacts doubled their effect and mutant hides properties were significantly buffed. Pseudogiant hide has as much bullet and rupture immunity as Medusa know along with other increased stats.

Hardcore Death Lamp [Demonized]
Doubles the price of Death Lamp but triples its radiation. You better sell this fucker fast now.

Powerful Attachments [vorcheski]
-Mutant parts are actually useful. Lower-tier animal parts provide much better protection than vanilla, and higher-tier animal parts are incredibly powerful. They should be!
-Outfit attachments like ballistic plates, surge protectors, camelbaks, etc. Were buffed considerably. Especially the armor plates, since most barely provided any protection at all. Stacking 5 of the carbon plates will make you feel like a god amongst men. Well, not really, but you'll be pretty strong!
-To counteract this, some of the negative effects were increased. You might be impervious to bullets, but take one wrong step into an electrical anomaly and you're toast. Literally.
-Changes similar to how the artefacts worked in the older games. A lot of the effects in anomaly are just flat out worse versions of outfit attachments. Some artefacts *barely* gave any bonuses at all. What the heck? This ain't misery son! Gimme somethin' good!
-I mean, that's it really. I did alter some negative stats to be more/less impactful. A lot of the psi damaging-related ones got nerfed, because with psi-stress it makes using the item a detriment.
