-Module:
Reenabled & half-enabled weapons patches

----------------------------------------------------------
-Compatibility:

Only tested for vanilla. May be compatible with reanimations that don't touch anything other than the gun itself, and not the distribution.

Other reenablers that affect the same weapons are redundant.

----------------------------------------------------------
-Explanation:

Some vanilla guns aren't disabled, but have some disabled configurations. Loadouts, trader, etc. This reenables some of these settings, with some expansions and integrations into mods as needed.


Gewehr 43:
Trader: restored to Bandits (basic) and Renegades.
NPC loadouts: it will be carried by low ranking loners, bandits, and renegades.

Integrated into:
Grok's Stash Overhaul
Lootboxes


M79:
Trader: Sold by factions that also sell the RG-6 at the one supply tier lower.
NPC loadouts: it was only found in the hands of Sin masters. Now it'll will be carried by Army, Clear Sky, Duty, Freedom, ISG, Mercs (including eco ones), Monolith, and Sin, with a 20% chance of being carried by masters and legends and a 10% by veterans. Army, ISG, and Monolith members with a +10% to their chances, Clear Sky and Sin members with a -5%.
As you can imagine, even with the low odds, this can be just as horrifying as Clear Sky grenata spamming.
