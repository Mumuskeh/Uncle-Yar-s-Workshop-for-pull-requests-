-Module:
Bullet Shell Sounds [Gabriel MacClay]

----------------------------------------------------------
-Compatibility:
NOTE: The original mod is still required. Let this overwrite it.

----------------------------------------------------------
-Explanation:

The last mod update recognized vanilla and BaS ammo.

It will now recognize ammo from:
-9x21 FMJ HP Ammo
-10mm Auto Ecosystem
-.300 Blackout Framework
-AO3 Late Comers
-Birdshot
-Ghost Armory .50 Beowulf
-LEWD Ammo Pack .50 BMG
-Neko's .30-06 Springfield
-Russian Ammo Expansion
-Small Caliber Ammo Framework
-Speedloaders and ammo for revolvers

Also recognized scopes from all mods covered by Pizza's Ultimate Sight Selection for Anomaly, and those in Joseph_Porta's Optics and laser sights.

A number of revolvers, explosive launchers, and pump, break, and bolt action guns from mods are also recognized, even with ID changes in this package.
