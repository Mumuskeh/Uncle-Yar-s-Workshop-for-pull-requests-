-Patches:

Allow Silencers
In v1.0, the mod allowed attaching a suppressor to each shotgun. This was removed in v1.1.
Still, all models still have a suppressor in their models. This allows to reenable them in a case by case basis. The vanilla PBS1 silencer will be used.
I'm sure Sedzhimol has a perfectly valid reason, I don't think this will break your game, but use with caution just in case.
