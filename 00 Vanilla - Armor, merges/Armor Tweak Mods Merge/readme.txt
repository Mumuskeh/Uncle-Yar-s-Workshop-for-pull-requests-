-Module:
Armor Tweak Mods Merge

----------------------------------------------------------
-Compatibility:
Original mods not required.

Incompatible with full armor system overhauls like Breaker_Oak's.

----------------------------------------------------------
-Explanation:

Merges different mods patched in this pack:
-Vanilla Fixes (DLTX MiniMod)
-Outfit Revision (DLTX MiniMod)
-Built Different
-Make Clear Sky Great Again
-Overcoats reworked
-Outfits HUD Arms Fix
-Consistent Head Protection

You could consider this an armor overhaul. If you squint real hard while singing The Girl from Ipanema.

The tweaks for exo suits are removed (not disabled), for compatibility with the more comprehensive Connelly's Universal Nosorog Tweaks.

The wpn_axe3's heavy attack fix left over from the original Vanilla Bug Fix Pack is retained. Pretty harmless, but if you use Barry's More Melee Features, you'll want to disable lines 2 and 3 of the file.

The Separated Helmets option skips or removes the head protection tweaks.

Includes the changes in the individual patches in this pack.
