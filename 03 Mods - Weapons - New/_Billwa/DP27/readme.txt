-Module:
DP27 [Billwa]

----------------------------------------------------------
-Compatibility:
NOTE: You still need the original mod. Disable the original configs folder.

----------------------------------------------------------
-Explanation:

NPC loadout and trader redistribution. It's an old, low tier LMG, it can stand to be sold at mid goodwill by all traders, and be carried by veterans instead of masters.
Removed from Army NPCs, who should have better things even at their worst, but traders will still sell it at low goodwill as in "no big guns for you rookie, unless you want the museum piece". Given to Renegades, who everyone always forget about (so to them go the scraps ho ho ho).

Integrated into:
Lootboxes
Grok's Stash Overhaul
PDA Interactive
Weapon Parts Overhaul

----------------------------------------------------------
-Patches:

Sound performance patch
This will reduce the number of sound layers used at the cost of sound quality, in case shooting the gun cause a performance drop for you.
