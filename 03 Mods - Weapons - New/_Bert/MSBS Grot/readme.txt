----------------------------------------------------------
-Explanation:

Description text edited up a bit, and added a short name for inventory purposes.
NPC loadouts. The mod attempts to give the gun to "professional" ranks, which inherit from the experience and lack their own weapon sections. So I give them to the experienced ranks instead. Ammo/scope configurations for the lower faction ranks have also been reduced, to avoid easy drops of AP rounds and high end scopes.
Icon file made DX8-9 compliant.

Tweaked actor effects and Grok's Enhanced Recoil settings. Instead of taking from the RPK, the gun will take from the HK416.

Mags Redux patch integrated.

Integration into:
Lootboxes
Grok's Stash Overhaul
PDA Interactive
Weapon Parts Overhaul

----------------------------------------------------------
-Patches:

Sound performance patch
This will reduce the number of sound layers used, in case shooting the gun cause a performance drop for you.
