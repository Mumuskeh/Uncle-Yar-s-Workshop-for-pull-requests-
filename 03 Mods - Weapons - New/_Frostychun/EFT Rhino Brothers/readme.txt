-Compatibility:
NOTE: You need the original mod. Let this overwrite it.

----------------------------------------------------------
-Explanation:

Internal IDs renamed, because my nascent OCD was screaming in my ear.
NPC distribution. The mod adds the gun to legend rank stalkers, but only a couple factions have those. So I switch it to master rank, and swap Freedom for Duty (which feels a better option due to the ammo being Russian and not NATO).
Trader distribution. It will be sold by factions whose NPCs carry.
Icon file trimmed.
Actor effects and Grok's Recoil configs tweaked to follow a VSSK, not a Beretta (different calibers and all that).

Integration into:
Grok's Stash Overhaul
Lootboxes
PDA Interactive
Weapon Parts Overhaul

-Patches:

Revolver Parts
Cylinder and hammer are assigned as parts.

Speedloaders and ammo for revolvers
The 9mm and .357 revolvers are assigned speedloaders.
This requires the Speedloaders and ammo module in this package, as it includes a 6 rounds 9mm speedloader which the main mod doesn't.
