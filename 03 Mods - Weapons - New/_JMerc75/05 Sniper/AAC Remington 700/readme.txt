-Explanation:

Internal IDs renamed, to better inventory sorting among other Remington 700.
Disabled the use_aim_bullet value (basically, first bullet shot does extra gibblets, but the whole game and modding ecosystem has given up on it).
Removed the inheritance of Karabiner sounds, given it later states values for BaS sounds for ToZ106, DVL and SCAR. Weird thing, that.
Swapped from the upgrades used for the SVD to the ones actually used for the Remington 700.
Actor effects and Grok's Enhanced Recoil configs tweaked to follow the Remington 700, not the Mosin. (like. Seriously? Guys? Seriously, guys? Guys, seriously!? SRSLY GUIZ!? :_D )
