-Module:
M1-M1A1 Carbine Pack [JMerc]

-Compatibility:
Let this overwrite the original mod.

Due to changes made to internal IDs, the patch is safe in savegames with the mod already present EXCEPT if you are also using Mags Redux, and you may risk crashes. Start a new game or wait until you do to apply this patch.
