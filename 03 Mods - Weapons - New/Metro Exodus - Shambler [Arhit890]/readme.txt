-Explanation:

English text edited.
Properly assigned the "shotgun" weapon class and a misfire probability, and corrected repair type from "pistol" to "shotgun".
Misfiring stats increased just a little. But the condition decrease per shot is maintained (it's quite high enough already).
Typo on the HUD FOV setting fixed.
The gun now loads all kinds of 12 gauge shells. Couldn't see much of a reason why limit it to buckshot. Let the kids waste slugs if they want.
NPC loadouts redistributed. Monolith won't carry it anymore; instead anyone below the rank of veteran in poor factions (and zombies) will do, but novices won't load slugs. The assignment to novice loners is fixed.
Trader redistribution. Fixed assignment configurations. Only south/poor faction traders will sell it.

Icon file reduced. Could actually use a redo of the icon itself but eh.

Integrated into:
Grok's Stash Overhaul
PDA Interactive
Weapon Parts Overhaul. Treated as a semi auto shotgun. Feels like should be treated as a revolver, but revolvers don't jam with WPO, and this gun has jamming animations.
