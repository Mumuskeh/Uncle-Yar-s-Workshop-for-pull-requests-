-Module:
BAS Extra Weapons [TheMrDemonized]

----------------------------------------------------------
-Compatibility:
The module is technically standalone, except for the Rowan loadouts patch, which we don't touch here. Still, leave your ratings in the original mod.

----------------------------------------------------------
-Explanation:

The weapons now inherit from others (or inherit correctly, or without repeating data) as it seem it was intended: the Saiga20 for the Saiga Eco, though I may change it to vanilla Saiga later; the TOZ 134 from the TOZ 34; and the Vityaz for the PP-19 Bizon-2-07.
This should also ensure visual consistency when using a rework or reanimation for those guns.
Experienced stalkers that carry Saiga 20 may have random sights and ammo (in case you managed to get your hands on the elusive Momopate's 20x70 slugs).
The full length TOZ 134 also uses the upgrades intended for the full length TOZ 34.
Icon file trimmed.

Added default actor effects configs.

The Mags Redux patch has been integrated.
Added a compact and extended 20x70 Saiga mags. The compact holds 5 shells and becomes the "default" mag of the Saiga 20; the standard and extended mags now hold 10 and 14 shells, 2 more than those for the Saiga 12 (smaller shell). A compact Saiga 12 gauge mag is also added. All 12 and 20x70 mags can be retooled between themselves, just for the heck.
