----------------------------------------------------------
-Compatibility:
NOTE: You need the original mod. Disable the original configs folder.

The mod is perfectly compatible with Billwa's version, and no particular compatibility work is needed.

-Explanation:

Both Kromo and Black variants are essentially the same. The real gun can switch between pump and semi auto fire, but it seems that can't be done in this game without serious fuckery. So instead the Kromo is now a bit lighter than the Black and degrades slower (think of it as not being painted or something Iunno).

The weapons had no npc loadout or trader distribution. They're now distributed to CS, Freedom, Mercs, and Monolith, and their experienced and veteran members.
