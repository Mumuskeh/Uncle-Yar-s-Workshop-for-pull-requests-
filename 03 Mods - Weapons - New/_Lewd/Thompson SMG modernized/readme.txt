-Patches:

No scopes comp patch
Most of the scopes included aren't sold, and some (not all) are already included in other mods like PUSSY.
This will disable these scopes to avoid redundancies and potential crashes.

Mags Redux - Share with Tommy Gun Drop
This will allow the modern Thompson to use the 1921 drum mag from Tommy Gun drop, and that one to use the modern's simple mag.
The M1A1 mag is not affected (its mag well was made different for cheapness sake).
