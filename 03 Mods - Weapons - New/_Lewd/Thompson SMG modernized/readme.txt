-Patches:

Model tweaks
Removes the laser module from the upgraded models. Three reasons why:
1) Taste. Maybe you don't want laser pointers and related tacticoolznez in your STALKER.
2) Performance. Sometimes the meshes used for the pointer itself can account for a third or even half the filesize of the weapon (!!!).
3) Already cleaner models for later modifications.

No scopes comp patch
Most of the scopes included aren't sold, and some are included in other mods like PUSSY.
This will disable these scopes to avoid redundancies and potential crashes.
