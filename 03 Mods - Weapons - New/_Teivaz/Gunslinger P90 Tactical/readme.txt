-Module:
Gunslinger P90 Tactical [Teivaz]

----------------------------------------------------------
-Compatibility:
NOTE: You still need the original mod (if you can find it). Disable the folders items and loadouts in gamedata/configs, and the gamedata\meshes\anomaly_weapons\wpn_p90 folder.

----------------------------------------------------------
-Explanation:
This abomina-ahem, mod has been removed from Moddb, but in case you still have it or find it, you can use this.

This weapon inherited the data of the base P90. To ensure compatibility with other mods, the P90 Tactical has been independized away.

It also didn't work NPC loadouts like every other kid. It does it now by DLTX, because potatoes.

It also adds it to traders. To accommodate it in the higher supply tier, the normal P90 is moved down one supply lower.

-Patches:

Model tweaks
Removes the laser module from the tactical model. Three reasons why:
1) Taste. Maybe you don't want laser pointers and related tacticoolznez in your STALKER.
2) Performance. Sometimes the meshes used for the pointer itself can account for a third or even half the filesize of the weapon (!!!).
3) Already cleaner models for later modifications.

Boomsticks and Sharpsticks
Cleaned up files to use the BaS scopes.
