-Module:
HK433 [Mik]

----------------------------------------------------------
-Compatibility:
NOTE: The original mod is still needed. Disable the configs folder.

Do not use with Sedzhimol's HK433.

----------------------------------------------------------
-Explanation:

Standardized the stats for all variants. They're all the same gun in different colors. And internally, the variants inherit directly from the main one.
Fixed filenames for actor effects and Grok's Enhanced Recoil configs, and made sure the variants benefit of it. Parts as well.
Trader distribution. Allows selling to traders.
Reworked descriptions to mention the particular painting and remove the grenade launcher indication, as the weapons aren't actually prepared for it.
Icon file trimmed.

Integration into:
Lootboxes
Grok's Stash Overhaul
Mags Redux
PDA Interactive
Weapon Parts Overhaul

----------------------------------------------------------
-Patches:

No anime variant
The third variant with anime decals is removed, for whoever cringes at the idea of lolis or whatever in their guns. All three variants are practically the same so it's not like anything is missed.

Honey Badger and .300 BLK Framework [Pillii]
Turns the black variant into a HK437, SMG in .300 Blackout. It will have a shorter range than other options in these patches, but higher muzzle velocity.
You won't notice the size difference, but uuuuhhhh someone else can do the modeling and animation job.
