-Explanation:
NPC loadouts fixed so it spawns as a secondary weapon and not primary, and limited to experienced and veteran ranks.
Raised trader goodwill requirement one level.
Added default actor effects settings.
Icon file trimmed down.

Mags Redux support:
The original was weirdly configured, assigning magazines from BaS' PL-15 while having its own P365 load type.
Now, it will have its own sets of mags. A 10 round mag, two more standard mags of 17 and 21, a extended of 32, and a drum of 50.
Technically, SIG also offers two other mags with 12 and 15 rounds loads, and ProMag a 30 round drum, but adding those seemed like bloating the set and this way each jump, from 10 to 17, and from 21 to 32, is more meaningful.
The drum is just there for silliness points.
