-Module:
Outfits Affect Movespeed [ahuyn]

----------------------------------------------------------
-Compatibility:
Make sure you use the last Github version of OAM.
https://github.com/ahuyn/anomaly-OAM

----------------------------------------------------------
-Explanation:

Vanilla faster protoexos tweak
This makes protoexos (but not exoskeletons or nosorogs) move a bit faster.
The idea is that the servos should, you know, be doing their job to raise your lifting capacity, not make you drag your ass like exoskeletons and nosorogs.
